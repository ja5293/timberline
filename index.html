What's next?


"In the afterlife, you are treated to a generous opportunity: you can choose whatever you would like to be in the next life."

You can be a majestic horse with great power and strength. You can be a giraffe to have great height. You can have the stealth and speed of a jaguar. Or you can be among your pack of wolves.

Click on the picture that you would like to pursue in your destination to the afterlife. Click the mystery box if you want a surprise. It will take you to your new journey in a new form.
